About Mountain Life

Welcome to Mountain Life, a blog born from my passion for front-end development and love for the great outdoors. This blog is a result of my exam assignment (project exam 1) in front-end development studies. The blog revolves around Kvitdalen which is part of Folldal municipality. Folldal is an area I have hiked in a lot throughout my life and is an area with incredibly beautiful nature.

At Mountain Life, I invite you to embark on a virtual journey through the breathtaking views of Kvitdalen, a hidden gem teeming with cabins, majestic mountains, and outdoor activites waiting to be explored.

The developer and storyteller behind this website is me, Kine. I am 25 years, from Folldal. I'm thrilled to bring you along on my adventures through my blog.
